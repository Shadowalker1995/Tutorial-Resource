During the last few months, I talked to many of my Data Scientists friends and colleagues who were job searching, and found three problems they shared.


First, job searching takes a LOT of time.

Second, once you start, you can’t stop. Offers are changing all the time!

Third is failing to meet all job requirements. Often, it’s just one that’s missing!


To address these issues, I built Scooby AI - a personal assistant to help your job searching process.

Here’s how:

- You build a profile

. - Scooby AI searches for the best job positions for you.

- He delivers them biweekly to your inbox.

- If necessary, he also sends curated study materials to help you land that dream job!


He’s currently equipped to help Data Science, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence folks. Due to recent developments, all potential job positions are remote!
